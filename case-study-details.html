About

  • As the global leader in LPG supply & solutions in India, Supergas leads the pack in LPG services, sustainability, safety, D & I and innovation.
  • SCM YUGA implemented SAP Transportation Management System in 9 Months of time.
  • The Project was India’s first SAP TM 9.6 implementation, that went live in December 2022 with rollout to 21 plants of SUPERGAS
  • SAP TM 9.6 system is hosted on Microsoft Azure and integrated with TITO (Truck-in-Truck Out) and VTS (Vehicle Tracking System) – a Mobility Solution offered by Sensel Telematics for Vehicle Tracking.
  • The vision of this engagement was to utilize TM optimizer for all regions for automated planning., as all the plants of Supergas had their own ways in terms of Business Operation and Planning.
